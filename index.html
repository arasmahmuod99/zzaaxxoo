    History of Zakho

    The first important historical event that took place in the city of Zakho dates back to the year 401 BC when ten thousand Greek mercenaries retreated during their retreat and passed through the Zakho Gate (Darband Zakho), which was called the Return of the Ten Thousand or the Zenfon Campaign.
